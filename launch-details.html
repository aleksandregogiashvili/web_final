Crew-5 Mission

The SpaceX Crew-5 mission is a NASA Commercial Crew Program flight to the International Space Station (ISS). It launched on October 5, 2022, carrying four astronauts to the ISS for a long-duration mission.

Mission Details

  • Launch Date: October 5, 2022
  • Launch Vehicle: SpaceX Falcon 9
  • Spacecraft: Crew Dragon Endeavour
  • Crew Members: Nicole Mann, Josh Cassada, Koichi Wakata, Anna Kikina
  • Mission Duration: Approximately 6 months

For more information, visit the official NASA page: NASA Crew-5 Mission.
